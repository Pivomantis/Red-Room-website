RedRoom is a party concept brought to you by IIAM & Happening founded in April 2016. The RedRoom team consists of new, committed, experienced organisers and promoters who's vision is to enhance the nightlife of The Hague through unique parties for the lovers of underground music. RedRoom events will be nights full of musical diversity, red colour dominance, heaps of smoke and a collective feeling of electric energy. Enter the RedRoom. Red is the magic.
